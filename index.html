SOLITUDE

INTRODUCTION


Loneliness can be a challenging experience, particularly for girls who feel isolated and disconnected from others. It is possible to feel happy even during periods of loneliness. In this blog post, I will share my personal experiences that have helped me find happiness and fulfillment when I was isolated from others.

Lonliness


Growing up as an only child, I had the opportunity to develop independence. While some may assume that being an only child leads to loneliness, I was fortunate to have constant support and companionship from my loving family members. Despite occasional moments of solitude, I never felt solitude because I discovered the power of self-entertainment. Engaging in activities and games brought me immense joy and kept me happily occupied. During my school years, I was fortunate to always have the company of my family and friends. Loneliness was not a familiar feeling to me during that time. I cherished the moments spent with loved ones. Although I had a cousin, our age difference meant that we didn't always play together. While we enjoyed occasional playtime, he found greater happiness when surrounded by peers of his own age, I cherished the moments we spent together. However, a few years ago, he tragically passed away, leaving me deeply saddened by his absence. In the midst of grief, I found solace in believing that he was still with me in spirit, even though he had disappeared physically. With his memory as a guiding light, I found the strength to carry on and make my family proud of the person I was becoming.

STEPPING INTO INDEPENDENCE


When the time came for me to pursue my higher studies, I found myself in a situation where I had to leave my family and friends. This transition was not easy, and I initially felt a sense of loneliness and longing for a companion. But, I soon realized that this is a period of opportunity for my personal growth and self-discovery. I started to explore my area of interest. When I learn something in my area of interest that was a special feeling not explained by words.

SELF-CARE AND SELF-LOVE


When I was alone, I realise the beauty of self-love and self-care. I started appreciating my work I realized that by acknowledging and appreciating my work, I was motivated to strive for greater things in the upcoming days. The love and appreciation I extended to myself brought happiness that made me feel like the happiest person in the world.
